Case Study 02

Enterprise E-Commerce
Performance Refactoring

Client

Regional Retail Conglomerate (Cognilearn Incubator Project)

Problem

The client's legacy monolithic architecture was resulting in severe latency during peak sales events, causing high cart abandonment rates.
